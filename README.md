---

## Core Thesis

The biggest bottleneck in neurodivergence care is not therapy demand.

It is the shortage of trained, job-ready human talent.

Most companies are building:
- therapy apps
- telehealth platforms
- clinic software
- therapist productivity tools

I am exploring the infrastructure required to:
- train
- verify
- simulate
- upskill
- deploy

the next generation of emotionally intelligent care workers.
